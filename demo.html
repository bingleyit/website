frequently asked questions
Is cloudpad free?
Yes! cloudpad has a generous free plan that includes tasks, notes, projects, kanban, travel planner, calendar view, and sharing. Pro features like the finance tracker, news reader, and mood music require a Pro subscription at $2/month.
Is my data secure?
Your notes are stored encrypted in transit and at rest. You can also enable end-to-end AES-256 encryption in Settings → Security, which means only you can read your notes — not even us.
Can I use cloudpad offline?
cloudpad is primarily a web app and requires a connection to sync. We're working on offline support for a future update.
How do I share a note or list?
Click the share icon on any pad to get a shareable link, or enter a cloudpad handle to share directly with another user. Shared pads can be set to read-only or collaborative.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
Go to Settings → Billing and click "cancel subscription". You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Can I import tasks from other apps?
Yes! Go to Settings → Integrations to import from Todoist, Things 3, Apple Reminders, TickTick, CSV, and plain text formats.
